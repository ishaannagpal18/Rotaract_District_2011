About Us

Rotary

Rotary is a global network of 1.4 million neighbors, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who see a world where people come together to take action to make substantial transformations â€“ across the globe, in the community, and within themselves. Rotary is a key player in fighting polio and pairs with the WHO, UNICEF, CDC, etc. to provide a staggering amount of vaccinations in the developing world. Rotary believes in providing service to others, promoting integrity, and advancing world understanding, goodwill, and peace through our fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders. Rotary significances diversity and honors the contributions of people of all backgrounds, irrespective of age, ethnicity, race, color, abilities, religion, socioeconomic status, culture, sex, sexual orientation, and gender identity.  

Rotaract

Rotaract is a global community of service clubs for everyone aged above 18 that encourages leadership and responsible citizenship, promotes high ethical standards in business, and fosters international understanding and peace.
Rotaract was established in 1968 by Rotary International, a worldwide organization of service-minded business and professional leaders. There are more than 7,500 Rotary-sponsored Rotaract Clubs in some 156 countries around the world, with over 173,000 members. It is a program of Rotary International. Rotaract Clubs work under the direction of their local Rotary Club. Rotaract majorly focuses on professional development, leadership development, and service projects. Clubs attract like-minded people from the community to help society.

Rotaract District Organization 3011

Rotaract District Organization 3011 is a district recognized by Rotary International which has been working for the Rotary's Areas of Focus and beyond, since 2015. The district spreads across the NCT of Delhi, the revenue districts of Faridabad, Gurugram, Rohtak, and Rewari in Haryana along with other Districts. It constitutes about 70+ Rotaract Clubs with 2000+ Rotaractors.
All these years, Rotaract District Organization 3011 has worked towards developing Rotaractors' personalities to make them fit enough to work for the welfare of the community, has increased the network and bonds of Rotaractors, and imperatively worked towards community service.
From creating an audio library for the blind to doing plantation drives, and from empowering local women to conducting Rotasia level events, the Rotaract District Organization 3011 has tried not to leave any stone unturned and shall continue to do such phenomenal work.
It also publishes newsletters, supports different clubs' projects, and organizes mega district conferences to bring in great stakeholders on one platform with the Rotaractors to deeply discuss the concerns of the society and the solutions, coming up through these discussions, into action. The district strives to continue this legacy of doing remarkable work and achieving more and bigger milestones every Rotary Year.
